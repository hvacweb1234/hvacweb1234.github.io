Your Trusted refrigeration and AC Experts

With over 18 years of experience in the refrigeration industry, my journey began with designing and manufacturing refrigeration equipment for major supermarkets like Tesco's, Sainsbury's, and Waitrose. I then transitioned into the hospitality sector, providing tailored refrigeration and air conditioning solutions to a diverse clientele, including zoos, colleges, hotels, and fast-food chains.

I then specialized further in various aspects of refrigeration, including handling larger equipment such as packs, water-cooled systems, and VRV units. Over the years, I've held roles ranging from service technician to managerial positions, gaining comprehensive knowledge and practical experience.

Drawing on this experience, I've founded Rel Refrigeration to deliver prompt and effective solutions for all your refrigeration needs. Contact us today to discuss how we can assist you with your refrigeration challenges. We look forward to partnering with you!
