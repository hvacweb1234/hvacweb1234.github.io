About Noble HVAC & Refrigeration

Noble HVAC & Refrigeration takes great pride in serving Northeast Wisconsin. We are dedicated to providing excellent service to both residential and commercial clients, offering a variety of HVAC and refrigeration services including commercial HVAC installations and repairs, residential heating and cooling solutions, as well as refrigeration and cooking equipment services.

Our services cover a wide range of heating, cooling, and refrigeration equipment, including:
  • Furnaces
  • Air conditioners
  • Mini splits
  • Boilers
  • Water heaters
  • Gas fireplaces
  • Rooftop units
  • Make-up airs
  • Exhaust fans
  • Air handling units
  • Walk-in coolers and freezers
  • Reach-in coolers and freezers
  • Commercial cooking equipment
  • Ice Machines
  • Ice cream machines
  • Slush machines

Ready to learn more? Don't hesitate to get in touch with us. Whether you have questions about our services, need assistance with an ongoing project, or want to schedule a consultation, we are here to help. Simply fill out the form on our Contact Us page, and a member of our team will get back to you promptly. Alternatively, you can just give us a call. We look forward to hearing from you and providing you with the exceptional services that sets Noble HVAC & Refrigeration apart.
