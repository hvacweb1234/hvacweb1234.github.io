About REL Refrigeration: Dependable Refrigeration Experts

With over 18 years in refrigeration, I began designing equipment for supermarkets before transitioning to managing refrigeration systems in hospitality venues like zoos, colleges, and hotels. My expertise expanded into larger supermarket systems, including packs and VRV units. Drawing from roles as a PPM engineer and manager, I founded REL Refrigeration to provide swift, efficient solutions tailored to your needs. Contact me anytime for expert assistance!
