Keep Your Undercounter Fridges and Freezers Running Smoothly:

Ensure your undercounter fridges and freezers remain in peak condition with REL Refrigeration’s repair services. We understand the critical role these units play in your kitchen operations and are committed to delivering efficient solutions to keep them running smoothly.

Common Issues We Address

1. Temperature Fluctuations
We diagnose and repair issues related to thermostats, sensors, or other components affecting temperature consistency.

2. Frost Build-Up
Our team identifies and resolves problems with defrost systems and door seals to prevent excess frost accumulation.

3. Compressor Failures
We investigate compressor issues, electrical faults, or refrigerant leaks to restore optimal cooling performance.

4. Water Leaks
By pinpointing the source of leaks and addressing drainage or component issues, we prevent water damage and maintain unit integrity.

Benefits of Our Repair Services

Extended Lifespan:
Timely repairs help extend the lifespan of your undercounter fridges and freezers.

Energy Efficiency:
Well-maintained units consume less energy, reducing operational costs.

Reliable Performance:
Ensure consistent performance and safeguard your stored goods with reliable unit operation.
