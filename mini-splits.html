What are Mini-splits?

Mini-splits, also known as ductless heating and cooling systems, provide a flexible solution for heating and cooling individual rooms or zones in a home.

These systems consist of an outdoor compressor unit and one or more indoor air-handling units connected by refrigerant lines. Unlike traditional HVAC systems, mini-splits do not require ductwork, making them ideal for homes without existing ducts or for room additions where extending ductwork is not an option.
